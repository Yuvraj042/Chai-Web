About Us

At Tapri Chai, we bring the essence of traditional Indian street chai to life. Our cozy space offers a vibrant and authentic experience where each cup of chai is brewed with love, spices, and a dash of nostalgia. Whether you're seeking comfort, warmth, or a taste of India, Tapri Chai is the perfect place to unwind and enjoy the timeless charm of a perfectly brewed cup.
